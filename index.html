Interested in joining our January 2026 batch?

IIT Madras, India's top technical institute, welcomes you to the Four-year Non-Campus Bachelor of Science (BS) Degree in Aeronautics and Space Technology.

Study from anywhere, at your own pace.

Eligibility

Anyone who has completed class 12 with Physics and Maths can apply irrespective of age, role or geographical location.

Coursework

Content, tutorials, doubt clearing sessions & assignments will be online, while quizzes, exams and labs will be conducted in-person at IIT Madras campus.

Flexibility

Learn from anywhere, at your convenience. Option is available to choose the number of courses per semester. Students can exit with a diploma by completing a given set of courses.

Upgrade to On-Campus

After Diploma, based on tests and interviews conducted, a select number of students who applied will be offered the option of attending all degree level courses on campus at IIT Madras in person.

To provide skill-based training in aeronautics & space technology so that the students learn the analysis and design tools that are commonly used in the related industry, along with the basic theory behind them. This training in design and analysis tools will enable those with a non-aero background to enter an Industry that is working in the field of aeronautics and space technology.

Why study Aeronautics and Space Technology?
This degree builds expertise in design of aircraft and spacecraft systems and subsystems. It prepares students for careers in aviation, defense, and space sectors with strong technical and analytical skills.
DEGREE PROGRAM

BS in Aeronautics and Space Technology

The main purpose of this degree program is to skill the students in the advanced analysis tools used in aeronautics as well as space technology. The courses are organized in the four main fields: Aerodynamics of Aerospace Vehicles; Flight Dynamics, orbital mechanics and Controls; Aerospace structural analysis; Gas turbine and Rocket propulsion. A capstone project leading to design of aircraft and space vehicles using all the tools developed familiarizes the students with the application of these courses to synthesize an entire system/ vehicle. There are 3 levels of courses in the program: foundation, diploma and the degree. After diploma, a few students selected based on their performance in a test and an interview conducted by IIT Madras will get an opportunity to attend the remaining classes in person on-campus. Rest of the students who meet the criteria fixed by IITM can continue in a hybrid mode towards acquiring the degree.

DIPLOMA PROGRAM

Diploma in Aeronautics

Students can exit with a diploma in Aeronautics from IIT Madras after completing courses up to the diploma level. The diploma would have familiarized the students in theoretical background and the basic analysis tools used in aeronautics in the fields of Aerodynamics, Structures, Flight Mechanics, Controls and Propulsion Systems including Solid Modelling, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) & Finite Element Analysis (FEA).

What is the Admission Process (Qualifier)?

You can apply for the Qualifier Exam by clicking on the link above.

You will get access to 4 weeks of course content.

Those who get minimum required scores in weekly assignments in the four weeks will get to write the Qualifier Exam based on the four weeks of content.

Those who get minimum required scores in the Qualifier Exam continue with the program. Others are encouraged to reappear for the Qualifier Exam.

Who is eligible to apply?

Anyone who has passed Class 12 (or equivalent) in any year with Physics and Mathematics can apply for the qualifier exam.

School students who have appeared for their Class 11 final exams (with Physics and Mathematics as their subjects of study) can apply. Those who qualify can join the program after passing Class 12.

What is the Structure of the Program?

The program is split into three levels:

  • 1: Foundational Level (32 credits)
  • 2: Diploma Level (52 credits)
  • 3: BS Degree Level (58 credits)

Total: 142 credits for BS Degree
